WELCOME TO Image Descriptor

We here describe images in form of a setence.Get started by uploading an Image

OUR Motivation

Our motivation was to take iamges as input and for words.We would further combine these words and make sentences which could be later be converted into an audio file.We developed this app to help the visually impaired. It converts an image into an audible sentence thus describing the view.

Know How

A brief story about how this process works, keep an eye till the end.

Step 1

Click select file and select an image from your computer.

Step 2

Click get descriptiom and wait for server to process the image.

Step 3

Voila!!! The app generates a sentence in form of text and audio.You can listen to the audio form of the sentence by clicking the play button.
